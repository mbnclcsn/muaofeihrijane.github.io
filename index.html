Years of serving the best cake and pastries

Proudly Filipino-owned, Eihri's started as a hobby-induced business in 2003. Our cakes would be lovingly shared frequently in get-togethers. Eventually, it turned to orders from family members who in turn shared their delight with a wider circle of relatives and friends. Once the cake boxes were adorned with a pink ribbon, its now iconic symbol, the hobby turned into a flourishing business. The rest is sweet history.
